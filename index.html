Intro

We all have an origin story, and I believe who we once were plays a role in who we are and who we aspire to be.

I was born overseas and spent my early years moving from place to place until my family settled in Pittsburgh, PA. I was homeschooled for the majority of my educational career, which required a large amount of self-discipline and dedication. This internal drive to learn and grow, I decided, would be the key to my success.

After a 33-year, ever-evolving journey to self-discovery, I found myself in the world of coding! Having completed a few free online tutorials, I was hooked and am pleased to say I haven't looked back. The ability to see your code come to life and solve real-world problems is an incredible feeling.

It also requires a level of attention to detail, patience, and perseverance that can be incredibly fulfilling. I am continually finding contentment in the challenge of creative problem solving. My drive to learn is stronger than ever, which is necessary for a field where there's always something new to learn! And the coding community is a supportive and collaborative one, which means there's always someone to turn to when you hit a roadblock or need advice - so I went to find my community. After months of research and interviews, I decided on what I believed to be the best and most immersive experience I could ask for, a coding bootcamp with Actualize.
